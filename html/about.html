About Pilot Wedge

Pilot Wedge was designed to provide simple tools to quickly access data in one place. The design aides decision making. It is a progressive web app which means you can save it to your home screen and use it offline, except for external links such as weather and some hyperlinks.
Weather Information

Pulls METAR and TAF for departure and arrival destinations. If ETD and ETA are entered, will give a quick synopsis of the conditions at ETD / ETA

Before Pushback

Simple checklist to ensure all requirements met before pushing back from the gate

Flight Rest Management

For 3-pilot crews determines rest blocks. Enter the Start rest time, arrival time, minutes prior to arrival time, and transition time. Quickly outputs pilot res periods.

Fatigue Risk Assessment

A decision aide to assess risk based on the Tritschler model for an individual flight leg. Company risk models make sleep assumptions; whereas this tool uses actual sleep. Each check is worth one point. Ten or more points indicate high risk. Risk mitigation steps could include adding naps, caffeine usage, flight leg modification, etc. This is NOT a go / no-go tool.

Substitution Considerations

This tool is for trip holders to aide decision making when determining to SUB or not to SUB. Entering the scheduled trip end and the revised trip end will provide a rough overage calulation based purely on TAFB. Entering the SUB window Start / End will provide a recommendation for how to handle your decision to remain in SUB or keep overage. There are many considerations and the decision to remain in SUB or not is up to the individaul pilot.

Deviation Banks

This tool will help track your deviation bank. You can enter your starting deviation bank / hotel bank. If entered correctly the tool should manage deviation bank. Knowing your current bank is half the battle. You can enter individual expenses for a month or sum them into one entry. The same goes for trip banks. If you earn hotel bank, be sure to update that information. This tool is still new, so some bugs exist. Also, information will be wiped if you erase your website history, so be sure to note your deviation bank. A future version will have the ability to save information long term.

Note: This is an informational resource. As always the decision to continue operations is up to the individual pilot.
